7 Ways to Reach Customer Service at Ryanair®: Phone, Chat, or Social Media – A Complete Guide

Ryanair’s main customer service numbers are:

  • US: +1 808-751-2262
  • UK: +44 203-900-3009

You can reach a live representative 24/7. Whether you’re dealing with booking changes, cancellations, or refunds, speaking to a live person ensures prompt resolution. Alternatively, use Ryanair’s live chat or email support.

Why Contact a Live Person at Ryanair?

  • Flight changes or cancellations: Get help adjusting or canceling your booking by calling customer service.
  • Booking clarification: Speak with an agent to better understand your reservation.
  • Refunds and compensation: Complex requests are best handled by a live representative.
  • Technical glitches: Quickly resolve payment or website issues by calling Ryanair support.

Ryanair’s Contact Options

1. Phone Support

Call +1 808-751-2262 (US) or +44 203-900-3009 (UK) and follow the prompts. Wait on the line to speak to a live person.

2. Live Chat

Go to the Ryanair website, navigate to the Help section, and use the live chat feature to talk with a real agent.

3. Social Media

Send messages to Ryanair on Twitter or Facebook for quick replies to general inquiries.

4. Mobile App

Use the Ryanair mobile app to call or chat with support without switching devices.

5. Email Support

Email Ryanair for non-urgent issues and keep a written record of your interaction.
